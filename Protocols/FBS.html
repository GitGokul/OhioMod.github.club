Stock Solution Prep:

  1. Label eight 1.5 mL epi tubes 0%, 1%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 50%, 75%, and 100% FBS (fetal bovine serum)
  2. Using clear RPMI 1640, dilute 100% FBS by making 1 mL stock solutions with 0%, 1%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 50% 75% and 100%

Experiment protocol:

  1. Add 100 uL of structure and 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to a 1.5 mL epi tube. Repeat this for eight total epi tubes
  2. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 30 minutes at 16000 G
  3. Carefully remove the supernatant
  4. Resuspend the structure by adding 20 uL of 1xFOB with 20 mM MgCl2 and pipetting up and down to mix
  5. Dilute the structure to 20 nM by adding the desired volume of 1xFOB with 20 mM MgCl2
  6. Add 100 uL of structure to each of 8 epi tubes with the following labels: 0%, 1%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 50%, 75%, and 100%. These concentrations represent percent of FBS
  7. Add 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to each tube
  8. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 30 minutes at 16000 G
  9. Carefully remove the supernatant
  10. Add 100 uL of previously prepared FBS stock solutions ( 0-100%) to the respective 1.5 mL tubes
  11. Let the solutions sit at room temperature (25 degrees C) for 24 hours
  12. Add equivolume amount of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to the respective samples in FBS
  13. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 30 minutes at 16000 G
  14. Carefully remove the supernatant
  15. Resuspend structure by adding 100 uL of 1xFOB buffer with 20 mM MgCl2
  16. Run an agarose gel so that the FBS percentages read low to high from left to right
