Stock Solution Prep:

  1. Label eight 1.5 mL epi tubes 0%, 1%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 50%, 75%, and 100% FBS (fetal bovine serum)
  2. Using clear RPMI 1640, dilute 100% FBS by making 1 mL stock solutions with 0%, 1%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 50% 75% and 100%

Experiment protocol:

  1. Label eight 1.5 mL epi tubes with the FBS percentages: 0%, 1%, 5%, 10%, 20%, 50%, 75%, and 100%
  2. Add 100 uL of PEG purified structure at 20 nM and 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to the eight epi tubes
  3. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 25 minutes at 16000 G
  4. Carefully remove the supernatant
  5. Add 100 uL of previously prepared FBS stock solutions ( 0-100%) to the respective 1.5 mL tubes
  6. Let the solutions sit at room temperature (25 degrees C) or incubate at 37 degrees C for 24 hours
  7. Add 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to each tube
  8. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 25 minutes at 16000 G
  9. Carefully remove the supernatant
  10. Resuspend structure by adding 50 uL of 1xFOB buffer with 20 mM MgCl2
  11. Run an agarose gel so that the FBS percentages read low to high from left to right
