  • PEG Purification


As a rule of thumb, 100 uL is the minimum volume of structure needed for PEG purification.

  1. In 1.5 mL tube, add the volume of your structure. Then add the same volume of 15% PEG8000, i.e. if you have 150 uL of structure, add 150 uL of PEG. Then pipet up and down to mix.
  2. Spin in the tabletop centrifuge at 16,000 g for 25 minutes. Resuspend in FOB + 18 mM or 20 mM MgCl2.
  3. Danny reduced 100 uL of structure and resuspended in 20 uL of buffer, but the volume you resuspend in is up to you.
  4. You're done! When making grids, keep in mind that the structures are now very concentrated. You may need to dilute them a little more to distinguish individual structures.
