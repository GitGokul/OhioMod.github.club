  1. In 1.5 mL epi tube, add desired volume of structure.
  2. Add equivalent volume of 15% PEG8000, and pipet up and down to mix.
  3. Spin in the tabletop centrifuge at 16,000 g for 25 minutes.
  4. Resuspend in FOB + 18 mM or 20 mM MgCl2, with volume equal to initial volume of structure.
