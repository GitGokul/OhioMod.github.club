Magnesium Protocol:


  1. Label eight 1.5 mL epi tubes with the MgCl2 concentrations:
  2. Add 100 uL of PEG purified structure at 20 nM and 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to the eight epi tubes
  3. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 25 minutes at 16000 G
  4. Carefully remove the supernatant
  5. Add 50 uL of previously prepared MgCl2 stock solutions ( 0-20mM) to the respective 1.5 mL tubes
  6. Using a nanodrop spectrophotometer, check the concentration of structure in each tube and dilute with respective MgCl2 buffer to get to 20 nM
  7. Let the solutions sit at room temperature (25 degrees C) for 24 hours
  8. Run an agarose gel using orange loading dye so that the MgCl2 concentrations read high to low from left to right
