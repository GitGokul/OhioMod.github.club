Magnesium Protocol:


  1. Aliquot 100 uL of 20nM structure and 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to a 1.5 mL eppendorf (epi) tube for a total of eight epi tubes
  2. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 30 minutes at 16000 G
  3. Carefully remove the supernatant
  4. Resuspend the structure by adding 20 uL of 1xFOB with 20 mM MgCl2 and pipetting up and down to mix (1xFOB consists of 1mM Ethylenediaminetetraacetic acid, 5 mM TisBase (Sigma 7-9), and 5 mM NaCl)
  5. Dilute the structure to 20 nM by adding the desired volume of 1xFOB with 20 mM MgCl2
  6. Add 100 uL of structure to each of 8 epi tubes with the following labels: 0 mM, 1 mM, 3 mM, 5 mM, 8 mM, 10 mM, 15 mM, and 20 mM. These concentrations represent the MgCl2 concentration in 1xFOB. Add 100 uL of 15% PEG (in 1xFOB with 500 mM NaCl) to each tube
  7. Centrifuge the epi tubes for 30 minutes at 16000 G
  8. Carefully remove the supernatant
  9. Resuspend the structures by adding 50 uL of 0 mM, 1 mM, 3 mM, 5 mM, 8 mM, 10 mM, 15 mM, and 20 mM MgCl2 to the respective epi tubes
  10. Pipet up and down to mix
  11. Dilute the solutions to 20 nM by adding desired volumes to the respective epi tubes
  12. Let the solutions sit at room temperature (25 degrees C) for 24 hours
  13. Run an agarose gel reading high salt to low salt concentration from left to right
